What is CredCheck AI?

CredCheck AI is a tool used to detect credible and fake news. It aims to stop misinformation spreading across the internet. This tool helps students, employees, researchers, and especially non-tech people to avoid fraud information.

What model is used?

The model used is Logistic Regression together with Vectorization within a pipeline. Logistic Regression classifies whether the news is fraud or not. Vectorization on the other hand, converts the text into a numerical format where machines understands it. This AI is trained with 40k cleaned data, having 98% accuracy.

How to use CredCheck AI?

1. Find a news you want to check.

2. Copy the heading and the body of the news.

3. Paste them inside the input field.

4. Click check and wait for the result.
